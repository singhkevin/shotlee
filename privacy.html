Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2025

At Shot Lee, privacy isn't a feature—it's a fundamental principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered image enhancement service.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

Shot Lee is built on a foundation of absolute privacy. We do not store your images. We do not sell your data. We do not share your information with third parties. Your memories remain exclusively yours.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (which is encrypted). This information is necessary to manage your account and credits.

Payment Information: When you purchase credits, payment processing is handled by secure third-party payment processors. Shot Lee does not store your full payment card details. We only receive confirmation of successful transactions.

Usage Data: We collect basic usage metrics such as the number of images processed and credits consumed. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve our service.

Images: We do not store your images. Images are processed in real-time and permanently deleted immediately after enhanced versions are delivered to you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Shot Lee service
  • Process your image enhancements
  • Manage your account and credits
  • Process payments for credit purchases
  • Send service-related communications (order confirmations, account updates)
  • Improve our service through anonymized usage analytics
  • Respond to your support requests

4. Image Processing and Storage

No Image Storage: Shot Lee does not store your original images or enhanced images. All processing occurs in real-time. Images are uploaded, processed, and delivered—then permanently deleted from our systems immediately after delivery.

Processing: Images are processed using our proprietary AI algorithms. During processing, images exist temporarily in secure, encrypted memory. Once processing is complete and enhanced versions are delivered, all image data is permanently erased.

No Backup or Archive: We do not maintain backups, archives, or any form of long-term storage of your images.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or images with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party service providers for:

  • Payment processing (stripe, PayPal, etc.)
  • Cloud infrastructure for real-time processing
  • Email delivery for service communications

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it solely for the purpose of providing services to Shot Lee.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Shot Lee, our users, or others.

6. No Advertising

Shot Lee does not display advertisements. We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not participate in advertising networks or data brokers. Your information is never used to target you with ads.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting employee access to user data

While we take reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@shotlee.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Shot Lee is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@shotlee.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users with reasonable advance notice. The "Effective Date" at the top indicates when this policy was last updated.
